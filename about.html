About The Collab

HEINZ BURGER KING COLLAB


A lot has happened on the way to becoming America’s Favorite Ketchup, and 150 years later, one of the worlds larget fast-food chains, Burger King has collaborated with Heinz to celebrate.

On February 1st, the first launch of the Heinz Burger King bottles came into stores across Canada.
