About The Collab

WHY THE COLLAB?


Burger King has teamed up with Heinz to make our own line of restaurant sauces, in a Heinz bottle which you can take home and use for your own liking.

The partership is a celebration of the 150 year anniversary of Heinz - Each special bottle comes with a unique Heinz and Burger King logo with themed styling.
